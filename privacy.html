3. How long we keep your data

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal obligations:

  • Transaction and financial records: 7 years (Companies Act 2006 requirement)
  • Operator account data: Duration of the contract plus 12 months
  • Driver session records: 7 years (financial record requirement)
  • Communication records: 3 years from last contact

4. Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the following processors, under appropriate data processing agreements:

  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing and operator payouts. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Supabase, Inc. — Database hosting and API infrastructure. Data stored in EU data centres. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — Website hosting and delivery.

We may also disclose data where required by law, court order, or to prevent fraud.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we use your data while a dispute is resolved

To exercise any of these rights, email amirvieyra@yahoo.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

6. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and hashed credential storage. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe under PCI-DSS Level 1 certification.

7. International transfers

Our primary data infrastructure is hosted within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where data is transferred outside the EEA (for example, to Stripe's US infrastructure), appropriate safeguards are in place under standard contractual clauses.
